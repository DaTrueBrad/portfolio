Career History

VASA Fitness

A large part of my work history comes from a company based out of Utah called VASA Fitness. They are a High Value Low Price (HVLP) gym model who is currently ranked in the Top 15 in the nation for fitness centers. It was my pleasure to work for them as a Personal Trainer, Sales Manager, and Fitness Manager over the course of three amazing years. The company structure helped me develop organization, learning, leadership, and drive. I loved helping out the members of the club through the Core Values UPLIFT, which stood for Unity, Passion, Love, Integrity, Fun, and Trust. I left the company becuse it was time for me to start a new chapter in my life using the knowledge I had been given the opportunity to acquire.
